Data Encoding

The basemap indicates the boundary of each community in San Francisco.

In the heatmap, the color diverging in boxes indicates the count of unique incidents in each weekdays per week on 2018.

  • The x-axis of heatmap is the incidents category: Assualt, Fraud, Robbery, Stolen Property and Suspicious
  • The y-axis is the weekdays: Sunday, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday and Sunday.

Interactivity

When you try to explore the incidents information for each community, all you need to do is just mouseover in the map. When you do that, you could see a heatmap in the right side of the basemap for demonstrating the incidents happend in each weekdays, the communities do not have same number of incidents, the heatmap will only present the incidents happend on the community.

Finding

I found that the number of assualt incidents is much more than other incidents category, it happened on all the communities, people should have cauction on this incidents category when they are hanging out with other people

I also found that the community closed to downtown like Mission, Tenderloin and Financial District has more number of incidents happened than other community. If you can see the population of San Francisco on https://statisticalatlas.com/, you could see that those community has more population than other community. The conclusion here: the more population the more incidents happened. When you hang out in a larage population district, you should be careful the incidents happen on you.
