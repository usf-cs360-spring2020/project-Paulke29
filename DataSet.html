This article gives an overview of the dataset Police Department Incident Reports: 2018 to Present which is available here: https://data.sfgov.org/d/wg3w-h783

Size of Dataset

Rows: 337K and Columns: 26

Columns Name Desciption Type
Incident Year The Year when the incident occurred Date&Time
Incident Date The date incident incurred Date&Time
Analyze-Neighborhood Neighborhood Plain text
Incident Day of week The day of week incident incurred Data&Time
longitude The longitude coordinate in WGS84, spatial reference is EPSG:4326. Note, will be blank where geocoding was not possible. Number
Latitude The latitude coordinate in WGS84, spatial reference is EPSG:4326. Note, will be blank where geocoding was not possible. Number
